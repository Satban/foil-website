The Opportunity

We've all opened the fridge to find forgotten leftovers, or discovered produce hiding in the back drawer past its prime.

It's not about caring less. Life gets busy, and keeping track of everything in our kitchens is harder than it should be. That's where Foil comes in.

How Foil Helps

Foil is your kitchen's memory. Scan a receipt and we track everything you bought. Get a gentle nudge when your strawberries are ready to use. When ingredients are at their peak, we suggest recipes to enjoy them.

Use more. Enjoy more. Simple as that.
