Our Values

Why we're building Foil and what drives us every day.

40% of food in America is thrown away.*

That's not just waste—it's money from your pocket, resources from our planet, and meals that could have been enjoyed. Foil exists to change that, one household at a time.

The Problem We're Solving

We've all been there: you open the fridge, find that container of leftovers you forgot about, and toss it straight into the trash. Or you buy groceries with the best intentions, only to discover wilted greens hiding in the back drawer a week later.

It's not that we don't care. It's that life gets busy, and keeping track of what's in our kitchens is harder than it should be.

How Foil Helps

Foil is your kitchen's memory. Scan a receipt, and we'll track everything you bought. Get a gentle reminder before your strawberries go bad. When ingredients are about to expire, we'll suggest recipes to use them up.

No more guessing. No more guilt. Just less waste.
