High-protein Kung Pao stir-fry

25 min · 36g protein · gentle on stomach
Already in your kitchen
Chicken breast · 1 lb
Broccoli · 1 head
Garlic · 4 cloves
Soy sauce · low sodium
Rice · 1 cup
Add to make it perfect
Ginger root · 1 inch
Sesame oil · 1 tbsp
Peanuts · 1/4 cup
+ Show 3 more optional ingredients
Steps
Cube chicken into 1-inch pieces. Toss with 1 tbsp soy sauce and let sit 5 minutes.
Heat sesame oil in a wok or large pan over high heat. Add chicken and stir-fry 4 minutes.
Add garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds until fragrant. Add broccoli, stir-fry 3 minutes.
Add remaining soy sauce and a splash of water. Toss in peanuts. Serve over rice.
