Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

This page describes how Neera handles information collected through the research survey at getfoilapp.com/neera. This policy applies only to the research survey and not to any future Neera product.

Plain-English version: It's just me building a thing. You're sharing health info to help me build it. I won't sell it, won't post it anywhere, will delete it on request, and will throw it out within a year if the project doesn't go anywhere.

What we collect

Through the survey, we collect only what you choose to enter, which may include:

  • Your nutrition focus, goals, and challenges
  • Approach you're taking (on your own, with a doctor, with a medication, etc.)
  • If you self-identify a medication path: the specific medication and duration, if you choose to share
  • Tools and apps you currently use
  • Your reactions to the concept and what felt useful or off
  • Pricing preferences and willingness to share data
  • Open-text answers in your own words
  • If you opt in: your email address, for follow-up interviews or beta access

We do not collect: precise location, IP address (beyond what Google services may log on their end), browser fingerprinting, or any identifier we'd use to track you across the web.

How we use it

  • To understand whether the Neera product concept is worth building
  • To inform what gets built first and how it works
  • If you opted in: to contact you about a follow-up interview or beta access

We do not use survey responses for advertising, do not sell or share them, and do not transfer them to any third party (other than the storage providers below).

Where it's stored

  • Responses are submitted to a Google Apps Script endpoint and stored in a private Google Sheet that only the founder can access.
  • Backups may exist temporarily in Gmail (notification emails to the project owner) and in Google Drive (the Sheet itself).
  • We do not run a separate database. Nothing is shared with analytics, ad networks, or third-party services.

How long we keep it

We retain responses until the earlier of:

  • Neera launches as a product, at which point we will give you the option to migrate or delete prior research data, OR
  • One year from the date you submitted the survey

After that, responses are deleted from active storage. If you ask us to delete sooner, we will.

Your rights

You can, at any time, email letsmeetneera@gmail.com to:

  • See the responses associated with your email (if you provided one)
  • Correct anything that's wrong
  • Delete your responses entirely
  • Opt out of any follow-up contact

For Washington State residents covered by the My Health My Data Act: the above rights also apply, and you may submit deletion requests at the same email address. Requests are typically processed within 14 days.

For California residents under CCPA/CPRA: the same applies. Neera does not sell or share personal information.

For EU/UK residents under GDPR: lawful basis for collection is your explicit consent (Article 9(2)(a) for special category health data). You can withdraw consent at any time via the email above.

Children

This research is not directed at anyone under 18. If you are under 18, please do not submit the survey.

Changes to this policy

If this policy materially changes before a respondent's data is deleted, we'll notify anyone who provided an email address.

What this is not

Neera is not a healthcare provider, health plan, or healthcare clearinghouse, and the survey is not a clinical or medical service. HIPAA does not apply. Information you share here is not medical advice, and we do not provide medical advice in return.

Questions

Email letsmeetneera@gmail.com. You'll hear back from the founder directly.
