See Foil in Action

From receipt to recipe in seconds. Watch how Foil turns your groceries into organized, actionable intelligence.

Explore Features
01

Snap a Receipt, Skip the Typing

Point your camera at any grocery receipt. Foil's AI reads every item, recognizes what it is, and adds it to your inventory automatically.

  • Works with any store receipt format
  • Recognizes abbreviations and codes
  • Adds items in under 3 seconds
02

Recipes from What You Have

No more staring at the fridge wondering what to make. Foil suggests recipes that prioritize ingredients you should use soon.

  • Prioritizes items expiring first
  • Filter by cuisine, course, and dietary needs
  • Save favorites to your personal cookbook

Track What You Actually Use

Partially used something? Foil tracks that too. Mark items as half-used, and get smarter suggestions based on what's really left.

  • Log partial consumption
  • Accurate inventory counts
  • Better recipe suggestions
03

Plan the Week, Not Just Tonight

Map out breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks for the entire week. Hosting a gathering? The party planner helps you build menus and scale for guests.

  • Drag-and-drop weekly meal calendar
  • Auto-generated shopping lists
  • Party planner for special occasions
04

Your Kitchen, In Sync

Create a household and invite your family or roommates. Everyone sees the same inventory, updated in real-time. When someone uses the last of the milk, everyone knows.

  • Shared inventory, updated in real-time
  • Activity log shows who added or used items
  • Up to 6 household members
