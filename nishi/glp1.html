Why Nishi exists

I built Nishi after watching friends and family start GLP-1 medications and run into the same question with no good answer: "What am I supposed to eat now?"

The old playbook doesn't fit anymore. Calorie counting, generic logging apps, "eat less of everything." None of it was built for the reality of GLP-1, where hunger changes, portions shrink, and some days food just sits differently.

Registered dietitians are great, but most people see one eight times a year. People need something that listens, learns, and shows up in the kitchen at 6:30, when they're standing in front of the open fridge wondering what to cook.

Nishi is exactly that. A companion that listens, learns, and helps you eat well, quietly, every day.

Satyam, founder

Built with care, not claims

Nishi is a wellness companion, not a medical service. We don't diagnose or prescribe, we don't replace your doctor or dietitian, and we don't sell your individual health data. We work with a clinical advisory board.
